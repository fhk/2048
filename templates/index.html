What's all this Machine Learning Hype? This site is a demo of what can be done with word vectors. Ever wondered how words are related to each other? Google created word2vec, this take a bunch of words and turns them into vectors. You can also download pretrained vectors that have been trained on large data sets, e.g. Google News. The well know example is turning king into queen by using arithmatic king - man + woman = queen.

How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. Tiles can always be merged along the horizontal and vertical axis. The resulting word is the sum of the word vectors of the two origional words, they merge into one!
